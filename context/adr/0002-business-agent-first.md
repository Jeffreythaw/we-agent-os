# ADR 0002: Business Agent First

## Context
Horizontal framework features do not deliver direct business value. The product value lies in the vertical applications.

## Decision
We pivot entirely to delivering high-value Business Agents (e.g., Service Report Agent).

## Consequences
Engineering resources are shifted from OS plumbing to building executable workflows and UI layers.

## Enforcement
Roadmap prioritization explicitly favors Business Agents.
