# Engineering Terminology

- **OS / Kernel**: The frozen, deterministic execution engine (`src/`).
- **Skill**: A discrete, bounded wrapper around a capability (API, Template, regex).
- **Knowledge Pack**: JSON-defined business logic injected at runtime.
- **Business Agent**: A vertical application (UI + Workflow + Knowledge) that solves a specific business problem.
- **Concept Backlog / Evidence Required**: Ideas (like dynamic context injection or Electron) that are intentionally deferred until overwhelming evidence proves they are necessary.
