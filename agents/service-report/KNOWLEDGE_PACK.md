# Knowledge Pack

**Domain:** HarbourLink (HBL) Routine Contract Services
**Equipment Types:** FCU/AHU (Fan Coil Units / Air Handling Units)

## Context
Service reports for HarbourLink require strict adherence to reporting standards. Each report must uniquely identify the service visit via a `referenceNumber` formatted specifically for the HBL project. Technician observations and photographic evidence are mandatory for all routine services to ensure contractual compliance.
