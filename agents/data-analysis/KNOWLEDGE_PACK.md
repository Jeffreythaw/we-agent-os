# Knowledge Pack

**Domain:** HVAC and Engineering Data Analysis
**Metrics Tracked:**
- **Temperature Logger:** Ambient and supply temperatures (typically °C).
- **Chiller Trend:** Approach temperatures, kW/RT, condenser water temperatures.
- **Water Test:** Conductivity, pH levels, inhibitor concentrations.

## Context
Engineers rely on these analyses to determine if equipment is running efficiently or safely. A water test with pH outside the 7.5 - 9.0 range can indicate scaling or corrosion risks. The agent's job is to flag these deterministic limits.
